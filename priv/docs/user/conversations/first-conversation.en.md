# Your First Conversation

## Starting a new conversation

Click **New Conversation** in the top left of the sidebar. A fresh conversation opens in the chat area, ready for your first message.

You can have as many conversations as you like. Each one maintains its own context, settings, and message history independently.

## Sending a message

Type your message in the input box at the bottom of the screen. Press **Enter** to send, or **Shift+Enter** to add a new line without sending.

The AI will begin responding immediately. You'll see the text stream in as it's generated, so you don't need to wait for the full response before reading.

## Reading AI responses

Responses are rendered with full markdown formatting. This means:

- **Code** appears in highlighted code blocks with a copy button.
- **Lists and tables** are formatted for easy reading.
- **Bold and italic text** render as styled text, not raw asterisks.
- **Images** generated by the AI appear inline.

If the AI uses tools during a response (like searching the web or running code), you'll see progress cards appear between messages showing what it's doing.

## Auto-generated titles

Magus automatically generates a title for each conversation based on your first few messages. You'll see the title appear in the sidebar shortly after you start chatting. This makes it easier to find the conversation later.

## Editing the title

If the auto-generated title isn't quite right, you can change it:

1. Hover over the conversation name in the sidebar.
2. Click the pencil icon that appears.
3. Type your new title and press **Enter** to save.

You can also rename a conversation from within the chat area by clicking the title in the header at the top of the conversation.
